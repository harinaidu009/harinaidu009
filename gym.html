🏋️‍♂️ Step-by-Step Workout Techniques

Step 1: Stretching

Start with 5–10 minutes of full-body stretching.

Step 2: Warm-Up

Do 10 minutes of light cardio (jog or cycling).

Step 3: Strength Training

3 sets of weightlifting (chest, arms, back, legs split by day).

Step 4: Core Work

Include planks, crunches, leg raises (15 mins total).

Step 5: Cool Down

End with 5 minutes of slow walking and stretching again.
